Bihar remained a part of the Bengal Presidency of British India until 1912, when Bihar and Orissa was carved out as a separate province.

MOTIHARI

Motihari, city, northwestern Bihar state, northeastern India. It is situated on the east bank of a lake, about 25 miles (40 km) southeast of Bettiah. Motihari was constituted a municipality in 1869. A major road centre, the city trades in oilseeds and has sugar-milling and cotton-weaving industries.
